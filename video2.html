Quick recap: 15 PFLOPS FP4 | 288 GB HBM3e | 208B transistors | Dual-reticle architecture

Who Should Use B300

Ideal For

  • LLM inference at scale
  • AI reasoning / chain-of-thought
  • Multimodal AI (vision + language)
  • Training large foundation models
  • Mixture of Experts architectures

Not Ideal For

  • FP64 scientific computing (only 1.2 TFLOPS)
  • Edge deployment (1,400W)
  • Budget-constrained workloads

Software Stack

Seamless compatibility. No rewrites needed.


Frameworks

  • Full CUDA 12 compatibility
  • PyTorch, TensorFlow, JAX
  • vLLM, SGLang, TensorRT-LLM

Libraries

  • cuDNN, cuBLAS updated
  • NCCL: NVLink 5 aware
  • NVFP4 support from day one

Deploy existing models on B300 instances. Add NVFP4 quantization for the biggest memory and throughput gains.

Five Things to Remember


1. 15 PFLOPS of FP4 compute. The fastest AI GPU available today.
2. 288 GB HBM3e. Fit 400B+ parameter models on a single GPU.
3. 2x attention acceleration. Purpose-built for reasoning models.
4. NVFP4: ~3.5x memory savings vs FP16 with ~1% accuracy trade-off.
5. You don't need to buy hardware. Use it in the cloud.
